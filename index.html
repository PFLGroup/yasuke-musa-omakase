The Philosophy

The truest respect for tradition lies in keeping it alive.

Our counter honors the foundational art of Edomae sushi — the meticulous aging, curing, and marinating techniques born in old Tokyo to coax the absolute peak of flavor from the ocean.

Tradition is not a museum piece. Driven by a passion for the rigorous, uncompromising standards of true Edomae style, Chef Musa infuses his combined experience and cutting-edge modern culinary techniques into this classic foundation. The result is a dynamic, evolving omakase journey that honors the past while boldly stepping into the present.

— Chef Musa
